ABOUT ME

I am a passionate data analyst/scientist with an immense love for data visualizations and analysis. I believe numbers drive decisions and that is right where data comes into picture. Having pursued Computer Science Engineering as Bachelors with a major in Database Management Systems, my interest for data and analytics developed while I was working for a Text Classification using Clustering project in 2011. I spent days playing around and analyzing the data, trying different codes and learning different implementations to the point that it no longer seemed like a task - it just became something that I wanted to do everyday!

I started working with Verizon in 2014 after my Bachelors as a systems analyst where I developed applications using machine learning and predictive analytics. I also worked on KPI and metrics analysis with Master Black Belt Six Sigma team and created dashboards and visualizations for various business processes. I got an opportunity in 2018 to work as a data analyst in Qube Cinemas Inc, where I led a team of 3 members in data analysis, creating visualizations and dashboards and market analysis of various industry models in digital media distribution.

“You never learn enough!” - I realized this with almost 5 years of working in the industry which is why I decided to pursue my MS in Data Analytics Engineering. Here I am, still learning and building better products and solutions everyday!
